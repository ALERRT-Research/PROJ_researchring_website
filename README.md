# Research Ring Website

This is the source repository for the [ALERRT Research Ring](https://alerrtresearch.org) website — the public-facing research division of the [ALERRT Center](https://alerrt.org) at Texas State University.

## About the Research Ring

The Research Ring produces rigorous, evidence-based research to support the nation's standard in active shooter response training and to improve the health and operational capacity of first responders. Research areas include:

- Active attack trends and databases
- Law enforcement training methodologies
- First responder health and wellness
- Public and law enforcement opinion research

## What's on the Site

- **Research Output** — peer-reviewed articles, books, book chapters, and reports
- **In Progress** — ongoing projects and anticipated publications
- **Grants** — current and past funding
- **Media** — news coverage and press mentions
- **Staff** — Research Ring team members
- **Work With Us** — collaboration and opportunities
- **Ring Resources** (internal, password-protected) — process guides, R/Quarto tutorials, and internal documentation
